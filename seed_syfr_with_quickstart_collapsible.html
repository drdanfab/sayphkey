Quick Start Steps

Instructions to Encrypt Your Seed Phrase, Passwords, Passphrases...

  1. Turn off web connection (The online indicator will switch to offline).
  2. Choose Encrypt
  3. Choose your Keyword
  4. Choose the position in the alphabet to add the keyword (default is 1)
  5. Enter the seed words in the proper order (if required) in the Input Box
  6. Run or It will run automatically if Auto-Run is checked

To verify accuracy:

  1. Click Decrypt
  2. Copy the encrypted words in the Output Box
  3. Paste them into the Input Box
  4. Run or Auto-run
  5. The original decrypted text should be in the Output Box.

SUPER IMPORTANT: Erase any decrypted text prior to reconnecting to the internet.

Storing Your Encrypted Seed Phrases and Passwords:

  1. Store your encrypted information in the proper order (if required) in any manner that suits your level of security.
  2. For example, you can keep them together or separate them into different places, move characters around, add numbers in place of letters, etc. Just keep track of your method as you will need to revert to the original encrypted text in order to decrypt and decipher.

SUPER IMPORTANT: Ensure that you don't store the keyphrase with the encrypted information.
